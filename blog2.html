Don't Give Up

October 20th 2018 by Jeremiah

Staying motivated to code by yourself isnt easy.Sense trying to be a self taught programmer Ive wanted too give up and quit mutiple times. In my last years of going school it wasn't better. I had trouble focusing on the classes and was struggling to keep my grades up and I went to my programming proffessor and asked If I had a future doing this career and he told me flat out too my face no.That moment had a lasting impact on me for many years and almost crushed my dreams of being a developer. But it was also the defining moment in my life as well. It lit a fire in me to prove to that proffessor and society that I was going to be eventually good at this. And if anyone comes across this blog post and struggling to stay motivated. I say don't give up. Go after the dream of being a developer and making your own brand and skills and do what makes you happy. Dont just chase the potentiall money, or your going to want too quit.
